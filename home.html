Our Story

R & M Supply Company was founded 1976 by Reed and Mary Cruit in Virginia Beach, Virginia. In the early days, we specialized in 10 key cash register supplies and have evolved over the years to supporting the state of the art multifunction and cash register machines that you see today. In June of 2019, R & M Supply merged with Total Business Solutions, an Office Supply and Office Furniture company out of Portsmouth, Virginia. With more variety and more solutions than ever, both companies are able to fulfill any need a customer may have. With a broad customer base of banks, restaurants, amusement parks, convenience stores, corporate offices, law firms, and medical facilities, we stock items for every customer so that they can get their items next day and at a great price!

The R & M Difference...

It is our who truly makes the difference. Whether it is putting away your copy paper or making your delivery fast on our route, we tailor our day to our customer's needs. We thank you all for working with us over the year and we look forward to helping grow your business for many years to come.
